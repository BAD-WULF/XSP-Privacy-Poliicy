Privacy Policy for X Spaces Plus

Last Updated: November 2025

Introduction

X Spaces Enhanced ("the Extension") is a browser extension that enhances the X (formerly Twitter) Spaces experience. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles user data.

Data Collection and Usage

What Data We Access

The Extension accesses the following data locally in your browser:

  1. Authentication Cookies
    • We access cookies from x.com, twitter.com, and pscp.tv domains
    • These cookies are used solely to authenticate API requests to X/Twitter's servers
    • Cookies are never transmitted to any servers we control or any third-party servers
  2. X Spaces Content
    • Live transcripts and captions from X Spaces you join
    • Emoji reactions from X Spaces you participate in
    • Space metadata (ID, title, media information)
    • All content is processed locally in your browser and temporarily stored in memory
  3. User Preferences
    • Your agreement to the usage warning
    • UI position and display preferences
    • Stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  4. Downloaded Audio
    • Space replay audio is downloaded directly from X/Twitter's servers to your device
    • No intermediary servers are involved

How We Use This Data

All data is processed locally in your browser to provide the following features:

  • Display live transcripts and captions
  • Show emoji reactions
  • Enable custom emoji sending
  • Download Space replay audio
  • Toggle recording settings for Spaces you host
  • Customize Space replay titles

Data Transmission

Important: The Extension does NOT transmit any data to servers we control. The Extension only communicates with:

  • X/Twitter's official servers - Using your existing authentication to access X Spaces features

Your communication with X/Twitter is subject to X's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored temporarily in your browser's memory while the Extension is active
  • User preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • No data persists on remote servers
  • All data is removed when you uninstall the Extension or clear your browser data

Third-Party Services

The Extension only interacts with X/Twitter's official services:

  • x.com
  • twitter.com
  • proxsee.pscp.tv
  • guest.pscp.tv

Your use of these services is governed by X's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Transmit data to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Store data on remote servers

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data processing happens locally in your browser
  • You can uninstall the Extension at any time to stop all data processing
  • Clearing your browser data will remove all Extension preferences

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Summary: X Spaces Plus processes all data locally in your browser to enhance your X Spaces experience. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data to our servers or third parties. Your data stays on your device and is only shared with X/Twitter's servers using your existing authentication.
